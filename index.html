About me


My name is Diego Paredes and I am a Photographer and Cinematographer. I am 20 years old, born and raised in Tegucigalpa, Honduras. I am studying Audiovisual and Advertisement Communication in Universidad Tecnológica Centroamericana (UNITEC) in my home country. I also have an associates degree in Digital Cinema from Tompkins Cortland Community College (TC3).

My work, besides basing on photography, is inspired by a multidisciplinary background that includes film analysis, illustration, audiovisual production and video editing. I have a good composition design and a great attention to detail.

I can do several kinds of photoshoots, outdoors and indoors. I have been the Director of Photography and Director of Lightning Department for various short films. I like to capture unique moments in my photos while capturing the essential feelings people demonstrate in those moments. Photography is my hobby and my job as well, which makes it the perfect duo.

I work with what I love to do; it's a dream come true.
